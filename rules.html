How to Play Kaun Banega Crorepati :

On each level you will be presented with a question with four possible answers. Only one of these is correct.

  • For questions 1-5, you will have 30 seconds to answer.
  • For questions 6-10, you will have 60 seconds to answer.
  • For questions 11-16, you will have no time limit.

If you have answered any question wrong,

  • Between questions 1-5, you will get Rs.0
  • Between questions 6-10, you will get Rs 10,000.
  • Between questions 11-16, you will get RS 3,20,000.

Lifelines of this Game:

  • 50:50:
    The computer removes 2 of the 4 answers, leaving one correct and one incorrect answer.

  • Ask The Expert:
    Ask for Expert's advice.

  • Audience Poll:
    The virtual audience votes on which answer they think is correct.

  • Flip the Question:
    If you receive a question you do not think you can answer, ditch it and switch. The KBC game will replace your current question with an alternative, selected at random from the same level.
